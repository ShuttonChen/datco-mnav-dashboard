What is mNAV?
The Modified Net Asset Value (mNAV) multiple measures how much the market values a company relative to its Bitcoin holdings. It captures the premium (or discount) investors pay for Bitcoin exposure through corporate vehicles instead of direct BTC ownership.
Formula
mNAV = Market Cap ÷ (BTC Held × BTC Price)
mNAV > 1.0 → Premium: Market values the company above its BTC NAV
mNAV = 1.0 → Parity: Company trades at BTC NAV
mNAV < 1.0 → Discount: Market values below BTC NAV
Relationship with BTC:
High mNAV periods historically coincide with BTC bull markets and strong institutional sentiment. A falling mNAV can signal that BTC price appreciation is outpacing company stock gains, or that market enthusiasm is waning.
